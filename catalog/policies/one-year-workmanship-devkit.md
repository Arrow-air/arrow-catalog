# One-year workmanship warranty for DevKit products

Status: draft; legal review needed before public launch.

Arrow-sanctioned manufacturers warrant that products are built to the Arrow manufacturing standard listed on the product page. Manufacturer warranties cover defects in workmanship or manufacturing for one year unless otherwise stated.

This warranty does not cover crashes, pilot error, misuse, unauthorized modifications, normal wear, consumables, or operation outside documented limits.

Normal warranty claims should start with the manufacturer that sold the product. Serious unresolved issues may be escalated to Arrow governance/forum review.
